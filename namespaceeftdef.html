logical, parameter eftdef::earlytimestability = .true.

Early time stability: EFTCAMB checks the stability of the theory at all times making sure that the choosen model is viable during radiation, matter and DE eras. It is possible to enforce stability only at late times (from EFTturnonpiInitial to today) by setting this flag to false. This choice will however make the results dependent on what one calls late time, i.e. the choice of EFTturnonpiInitial, so we advice to state it clearly when reporting results.
